# Manual Test Checklist

1. Add at least 4 cards in Create tab.
2. Study cards and mark known to pass 50% known rate.
3. Continue studying until all cards in the deck have been seen at least once.
4. Once known rate is 75%+ and round is complete, verify prompt appears asking to auto-generate.
5. Click Yes and confirm new cards are added immediately to deck without waiting.
6. Verify newly added cards show in Study and Manage tabs.
7. Reload browser and verify cards and prefetched stack persist.
8. Toggle known flags in Manage and ensure study order still behaves correctly.
